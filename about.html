About me

I am a Junior in Highschool trying to figure out what to do with myself before my best years run out.

Some of my Achievements include Founding the Edison High School Badminton Club, playing Varsity Golf for 5 years, and maintaining straight A's for all of sophomore year.

My hobbies include playing golf, gardening, playing/programming video games, bicycling, reading absurdist philosophy novels, and exploring science.

If nothing goes well for me (which is a common occurance) I plan on working at McDonalds until I get promoted to manager and living out the rest of my days on that sweet $60k a year.

However, my main goal is to get a respectable job like an electrician or a pilot or really any well-paying job because I have no preference nor passion to drive me to favor one position over another, move in with the love of my life, and live happily ever after.
