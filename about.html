About me

Some of my Achievements include Founding the Edison High School Badminton Club, playing Varsity Golf for 5 years, and maintaining straight A's for all of sophomore year. Some of my hobbies are playing golf, gardening, playing/programming video games, cycling, reading absurdist philosophy novels, and exploring science. If nothing goes well for me (which is a common occurance) I plan on working at McDonalds until I get promoted to manager and living out the rest of my days on that sweet $60k a year. However, my main goal is to get a respectable job like an electrician or a pilot or really any well-paying job because I have no preference nor passion to drive me to favor one position over another, bag a bop, and live happily ever after. The path I'm on currently sets me up for computer programming or web design gigs but every body wants those jobs, and everyone knows AI is coming for them, so that market is pretty bleak.
